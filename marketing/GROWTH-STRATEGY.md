## 3. Pillar 1 — Win discovery on Google Business Profile

This is 90% of your discovery, so it comes first. **Do these in order** (all in the Google Business Profile app / Maps → "Edit profile"):

### A. Lock the name (do this first)
Pick **ONE** public name and use it identically everywhere: Google, website, Instagram, WhatsApp Business, board outside the shop.
Recommendation: **`Gopiram Sarees`** (matches your domain `gopiramsarees.in`; "and Sons" reads retail, "Sarees" states the category). Do **not** stuff keywords into the GBP name (e.g. "Gopiram Sarees Wholesale Saree Market") — Google can suspend the listing for that.

### B. Categories
- **Primary:** `Sari store`
- **Additional:** `Clothing wholesaler`, `Women's clothing store`, `Fashion accessories store`, `Boutique`
Category is the single biggest lever for *which searches you show up in*. The primary category does the heavy lifting — get it exactly right.

### C. Full description, services, attributes
→ Copy-paste ready text is in **`gbp-assets.md`** (section 1–3).

### D. Photos — the fastest view multiplier
Profiles with 100+ photos get materially more views than sparse ones. Upload in this priority order:
1. **Storefront / signboard** (helps walk-ins find the door + Street-view match)
2. **Interior** (racks full of stock — signals selection)
3. **Product** shots — 15–20, one per saree type (silk, banarasi, georgette, party-wear)
4. **"Team at work"** — you/staff serving a customer (builds trust)
Add ~3–5 new photos **every week**. Freshness is itself a ranking signal.

### E. Post weekly (Google Posts)
GBP posts expire in ~7 days, so a stale profile looks inactive. Post 1–2×/week.
→ 10 ready-to-paste posts in **`gbp-assets.md`** (section 4) — enough for 5+ weeks.

### F. Q&A — seed it yourself
Add and answer the questions retailers actually ask (MOQ, GST bill, courier/transport, cash vs UPI). Post them from a second phone, answer from the business account.
→ Seeded Q&A in **`gbp-assets.md`** (section 5).

## 4. Pillar 2 — Reviews (the ranking flywheel)

**Goal: a steady drip, not a one-time blast.** 2–3 fresh reviews a week beats 30 in one day (Google discounts bursts, and recency matters).

**The system:**
1. Generate your short review link once: GBP → *Ask for reviews* → copy the `https://g.page/r/…/review` link (or a `bit.ly` alias for the shop).
2. **Every retailer who buys** gets one WhatsApp message the same evening.
   → Message templates (English + Hindi) in **`gbp-assets.md`** (section 6).
3. Put a small **QR code** at the billing counter linking to the review page — a printed "Scan to review us on Google" card.
4. **Reply to every review** within a day (templates included). Replies signal an active, trusted business and let you naturally repeat keywords ("Thanks for shopping our *wholesale sarees in Naya Bazaar*…").

Target for the next 90 days: **30–40 new reviews at 4.5★+.** That alone typically moves you into the local "map pack" for category searches.
