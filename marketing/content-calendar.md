# Gopiram Sarees — Content Calendar 🗓️

A simple, repeatable rhythm you can actually keep up with, plus a festival overlay so you never miss a selling window. Pairs with **[gbp-assets.md](gbp-assets.md)** (everyday posts) and **[festival-posts.md](festival-posts.md)** (festival posts).

> **Time cost:** ~20 minutes, 3 days a week. Everything else (review requests, replies) is a 30-second habit at the counter.

---

## 🔁 The weekly rhythm (repeat every week)

| Day | Task | Where | Source |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Mon** | Post #1 — offer / product | Google post **+** Instagram | 10-pack or festival post |
| **Wed** | Upload 3–5 fresh product photos | Google **+** Instagram | Your phone |
| **Fri** | Post #2 — new arrivals / story/reel | Google post **+** Instagram | 10-pack or festival post |
| **Daily** | WhatsApp the review link to every buyer that evening | WhatsApp | review message template |
| **As they come** | Reply to every new Google review within a day | Google | reply templates |

**That's it.** Two posts, one photo batch, one habit. Shoot photos once, post to both Google and Instagram (same image).

> During a festival window, **swap the Mon/Fri posts for the matching festival post** instead of the everyday 10-pack.

## ✅ The one habit that matters most
> **Every buyer → review-request WhatsApp the same evening.**
> Two–three reviews a week is the flywheel that lifts you into discovery search. Everything else is amplification; this is the engine.

---

## 📊 Monthly 5-minute review (last day of each month)
Open **GBP → Performance** and note:
1. **"Searches that showed your profile"** number — is it climbing from 128?
2. **New non-branded terms** appearing (e.g. "wholesale saree gwalior")? That's discovery working.
3. **Total reviews + average rating** — on track for 30–40 @ 4.5★+?
4. Which product category drove the most calls/messages → post more of that next month.
